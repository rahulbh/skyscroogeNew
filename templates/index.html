How would Ebenezer Scrooge save money on flight fares today? He would use SkyScrooge! :

  • SkyScrooge is the only travel website in the world that allows you to compare flight prices across different booking countries!
    • Airfares vary based on the which country you access a travel website in.
    • Did you know that flight prices can vary upwards of 10% depending on the country version of the website used? Well, Scrooge does, and he uses SkyScrooge to save his money spent on air fares.
    • SkyScrooge shows fares across different country versions of the SkyScanner website.
    • All fares and deeplinks for bookings have been verified by SkyScanner.
    • No more waiting for weeks for prices to drop by 10% as predicted by several websites and apps!
    • Be like Scrooge. Use SkyScrooge to visit your dream destinations with the cheapest airfare costs.
